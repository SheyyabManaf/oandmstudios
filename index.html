Good to Know

Frequently asked

How do I reserve my date?

A 30% deposit reserves your event date. The remaining balance is due 7 days before the event. We accept Cash, Zelle, Venmo, and PayPal — details are shared when you book.

What areas do you serve?

We serve weddings and events across Chicagoland. Travel beyond the area is available by quote.

What’s included with every booking?

A professional DSLR photo booth, an on-site attendant, unlimited photo sessions, instant prints, digital sharing, a custom photo template, an ivory backdrop, and full setup & breakdown.

Can we customize the backdrop or template?

Yes. A custom photo template is included, and an ivory backdrop comes standard. Custom or specialty backdrops can be arranged for an additional charge, agreed before the event.

What is your cancellation policy?

We’ll always do our best to accommodate changes to your plans — just reach out as early as you can and we’ll work with you. The reservation deposit becomes non-refundable if an event is canceled within 14 days of the date, since we hold that day exclusively for you.
